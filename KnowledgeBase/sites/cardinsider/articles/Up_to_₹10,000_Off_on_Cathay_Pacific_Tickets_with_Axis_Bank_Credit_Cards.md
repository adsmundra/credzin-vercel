# Up to ₹10,000 Off on Cathay Pacific Tickets with Axis Bank Credit Cards
16 May, 2025  / By  [ Rose Chugh  ](https://cardinsider.com/author/rose/ "View all posts by Rose Chugh") /  [Leave a Comment](https://cardinsider.com/blog/discount-cathay-pacific-tickets-axis-bank-credit-cards/#respond)
_If a premium vacation was on your 2025 bucket list, Axis Bank and Cathay Pacific have you covered. Here’s your chance to fly with Cathay Pacific and save up to_** _₹10,000 per ticket_** _when you book using Axis Bank Credit Cards._
Cathay Pacific is a Hong Kong-based international airline known for its comfortable cabins. Cathay offers a smooth flying experience with great onboard service, modern aircraft, and well-connected routes across Asia, North America, Europe, and Australia. Now, with this special offer, booking Premium Economy or Business Class flights becomes more affordable. The discount applies to**one-way or round-trip flights** departing from major Indian cities.
The offer is valid till December 31, 2025, so you have enough time to plan your trip, or even apply for a new Axis Bank credit card to grab this deal.
![Axis Bank Credit Cards Cathay Pacific Tickets Discount Offer](https://cd9941cc.delivery.rocketcdn.me/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Axis-Bank-Credit-Cards-Cathay-Pacific-Tickets-Discount-Offer.webp)
_The offer is valid for a limited time, but the good news is, it stretches till December 31, 2025. So even if you’re not an Axis cardholder yet, this gives you plenty of time to apply for an Axis Credit Card and enjoy savings on your next trip._
[Apply Now](https://cardinsider.com/go/axisbank)
## Understanding the Offer
Axis Bank credit cardholders can save as much as ₹10,000 **on each ticket** when reserving Premium Economy or Business Class one-way or round-trip flights from India to any destination on Cathay Pacific operated and marketed flights.
**Cabin Class** | **Discount** | **Promo Code**  
---|---|---  
Premium Economy & Business | ₹10,000 | **AXISBIZ**  
The offer started on February 10, 2025, and remains valid until December 31, 2025. The final date for travel is also December 31, 2025. The offer is valid for affluent Axis credit cards and select debit cards. 
When paired with travel-dedicated cards like the [Axis Bank Atlas Credit Card](https://cardinsider.com/axis-bank/axis-bank-atlas-credit-card/), which offer an accelerated reward rate for travel categories, the offer becomes even more valuable.
## Offer Terms
  *     * The offer is **_not_** valid on [Axis Bank Rupay Credit Card](https://cardinsider.com/axis-bank/best-axis-bank-rupay-credit-cards-india/) transactions done Via UPI.
    * Axis cardholders need to be Cathay Members and must sign in to their Cathay Member account to complete the booking.
    * Discounts are available for adult, child, and infant passengers on every ticket for the whole journey.
    * The offer is valid for one-way or round-trip flights departing from India: Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad.
    * **The discount code is not applicable for open-jaw, stopover, multi-city, package, or through the Cathay Pacific mobile app and other channels.**


For additional detailed terms and conditions, please refer to [this](https://www.axisbank.com/docs/default-source/default-document-library/cathay-pacific-tc-31-12-25.pdf).
## How to avail the offer?
  * Visit the [Cathay Pacific](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_IN.html) website.
  * Log in as a Cathay member.
  * Select eligible routes in Premium Economy or Business class.
  * Enter promo code: **AXISBIZ** at checkout.
  * Use your eligible Axis Bank Credit Card to complete payment.


## Bottom Line
This is a great opportunity to save money while flying in comfort with Cathay Pacific. With up to ₹10,000 off on each Premium Economy or Business Class ticket, Axis Bank credit cardholders can enjoy both value and luxury. The offer is valid throughout 2025, so there’s plenty of time to plan your travel. If you don’t have an Axis Bank credit card yet, you can still apply and make use of this deal. Whether it’s a work trip or a vacation, now is the perfect time to book your flights and enjoy premium travel for less.
